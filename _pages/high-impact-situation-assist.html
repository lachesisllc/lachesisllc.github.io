High Impact Situation Assist

Throughout the lifespan of every organization, certain circumstances can arise that will put your organization into what we refer to as a high impact situation. These situations require immediate and well thought plans and they often require resources that you may not have within your organization. Lachesis partners have been involved in many of such situations and can provide you with the focused and immediate recommendations you need.

Cash Flow Management

We will perform a detailed review of cash expenditures and prepare a cash flow forecast to give you a more clear picture of your organizations cash needs. We will offer suggestions to improve cash flow, manage working capital and reduce expenditures.

Operational Effectiveness

Working from your strategy, we will examine the activities within your organization that are crucial to your competitive position. We will gather data around these activities and provide you with visibility and insight into how these activities can be managed and improved.

Asset or Total Dispositions

Selling off assets or entire segments of your business requires careful planning and experts who are experienced in such transactions. We will manage the disposition process for you to ensure the best possible outcome.
