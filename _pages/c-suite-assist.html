C-Suite Assist

Lachesis Partners have many years of experience both in holding various C-Suite offices and in advising chief executives of other businesses. We have experience across numerous industries both domestically and internationally. Our C-Suite Assist services are designed to help executives and owners better manage their businesses across all key executive disciplines or to take a deep dive into specific initiatives.

CEO Assist

  • Deep dive to refresh the organization’s strategy around key differentiators
  • Customer assessment to determine how key customers make purchase decisions
  • Competitor and industry assessment to determine the organization’s competitive position
  • Review of governance procedures

CFO Assist

  • Building management dashboards to more efficiently manage data
  • Detailed financial analysis to assess key financial performance indicators
  • Working capital and cash management assessment
  • Talent assessment

CIO Assist

  • Analysis of how technology supports the business
  • Business Productivity & Efficiency
  • IT Value Proposition to the Business
  • Business Cost Reduction & Controls
